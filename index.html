GNTDA MADINA ZONE

GNTDA Madina Zone is a vibrant hub of fashion and creativity located in the heart of Madina, Accra, Ghana. As a part of the Ghana National Tailors and Dressmakers Association (GNTDA), this zone has been a cornerstone of the local fashion industry for decades.
