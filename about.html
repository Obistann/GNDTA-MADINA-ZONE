We Do What we love to do.

GNTDA Madina Zone is a vibrant hub of fashion and creativity located in the heart of Madina, Accra, Ghana. As a part of the Ghana National Tailors and Dressmakers Association (GNTDA), this zone has been a cornerstone of the local fashion industry for decades.

Rich History


The Madina Zone has a long-standing tradition of craftsmanship and excellence in tailoring and dressmaking. Generations of skilled artisans have honed their craft within these walls, passing down their expertise to new generations.

A Hub of Innovation


GNTDA Madina Zone is more than just a collection of tailors and dressmakers. It's a dynamic community that fosters innovation and creativity. The zone is home to a variety of businesses, including fabric suppliers, accessory makers, and fashion designers. This vibrant ecosystem encourages collaboration and the development of new trends and styles.

A Commitment to Quality


GNTDA Madina Zone is dedicated to providing high-quality fashion products and services. The artisans in the zone are known for their attention to detail, craftsmanship, and commitment to excellence. Whether you're looking for a custom-made outfit or a ready-to-wear piece, you can be confident in the quality of the products you'll find here. A Community of Fashion Enthusiasts Beyond its commercial value, GNTDA Madina Zone is a vibrant community of fashion enthusiasts. The zone hosts various events, workshops, and fashion shows throughout the year, bringing together designers, models, and fashion lovers from all walks of life. A Symbol of African Fashion GNTDA Madina Zone has become a symbol of African fashion, showcasing the beauty, creativity, and diversity of the continent's textile industry. The zone's contribution to the Ghanaian fashion scene is undeniable, and it continues to be a source of inspiration for designers and fashion enthusiasts alike..

GNTDA Madina Zonal was born from a passion for fashion and a desire to empower individuals through style. Inspired by the vibrant culture and rich heritage of Ghana, we set out to create a platform that celebrates local fashion designers and offers unique, high-quality clothing.
